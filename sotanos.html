Sotano’s is a cosy underground bar in York known for its relaxed atmosphere and strong focus on cocktails and tequila. With a warm, intimate setting and friendly service, it’s a popular spot for pre‑drinks or a laid‑back evening. The bar often hosts live music and DJs, adding to its vibrant yet welcoming feel.
